Guy Corem

Co-Founder of Spondoolies-Tech

Guy was a hobbyist miner using GPUs during 2011-2012. In 2013 he co-founded Spondooliee-Tech which was one of the first ASIC producing companies. Spondooliee-Tech produced the Hammer 40nm ASIC and the SP10 machine based on it and the 28nm RockerBox ASIC and the SP30, 35 and SP20 machines based on it.

Martin Caldera

OG Miner Since 2011

Martin has been involved with Cryptocurrency since the end of 2011, starting with helping build CPU and FPGA farms. In 2013 he began building farms with ASIC miners with some of the first ASIC some of which are Gridseed, Bitmain and others. Starting in 2015 he began building large scale mines with current ASIC technology with a background in Datacenter design and security. Since this time.he has built many successful mining operations.
